Instant Noodle

Description

Instant Noodles, a meal originating from Japan. It is a simple yet fulfilling meal found all around the world. It is easy to cook, making it the best midnight snack.

Ingredients

  • Instant Noodles
  • Seasoning Packets
  • Egg (Optional)

Steps

  1. Boil two cups of water in a pan
  2. Place the instant noodles in the boiling water
  3. Wait for three minutes and place the seasoning packets in the pan
  4. Crack the egg and put it the pan
  5. Stir until the egg solidified
  6. Remove the noodles from the pan and enjoy your meal
