Chicken Buffalo

Description

This is the famous Chicken Buffalo Wings, a savoury fried chicken coated with sweet, buffalo wings sauce. Chicken Buffalo Wings is a delicacy all around the globe, from the Oriental Countries all the way to Western Societies.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ketchup
  • Cooking Oil
  • Butter
  • Oyster Sauce

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken
  2. Beat the egg
  3. Coat the chicken with the beaten egg
  4. Coat the chicken with the flour
  5. Heat up the frying pan and place the cooking oil
  6. Fry the chicken until golden brown
  7. Repeat steps 3 to 5 until you fried all the chicken
  8. Let the chicken rest for 15 minutes
  9. Heat up another frying pan and melt the butter within
  10. Place the appropriate amount of ketchup in the pan
  11. Put the tablespoon of brown sugar
  12. Mix the Oyter Sauce
  13. Mix the sauce until the desired consistency is achieved
  14. Turn off the stove
  15. Place the fried chicken in the pan and coat with the sauce
  16. Enjoy your Chicken Buffalo
